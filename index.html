About Bellezza

Hi, We’re Bellezza Wedding Stories
Based in Sri Lanka | Available for Destination Weddings

Bellezza Wedding Stories is a well-known premier wedding photography brand dedicated to capturing timeless love with artistic elegance and heartfelt storytelling. Every frame we craft is a delicate balance of emotion, beauty, and authenticity — because we believe every picture has a story to tell.

Specialized in cinematic moments and candid intimacy, we document not just events, but the essence of each couple's life journey. From quiet glances to grand celebrations, our visual narratives are designed to preserve memories with sophistication and soul.

Let your love story unfold through our lens — where every detail is cherished, and every image speaks forever.
